## Story

As a ThreeDoors developer,
I want planning and task management view handling extracted from main_model.go,
So that task-related views are maintained in a focused file independent of other view groups.

## Background

The planning/task views (Planning, AddTask, Breakdown, Extract, Import, Snooze, Deferred) are a cohesive group — they all deal with task creation, modification, and lifecycle management. Extracting them reduces main_model.go by ~300-400 lines.
