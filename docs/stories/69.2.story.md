## Status: In Review

## Story

As a ThreeDoors developer,
I want source and sync view handling extracted from main_model.go,
So that the 7 source-related views are maintained in a focused file without affecting the rest of the TUI.

## Background

The source/sync views (Sources, SourceDetail, SyncLog, SyncLogDetail, ConnectWizard, Disconnect, Reauth) are a cohesive group — they all deal with external data source management. Extracting them as a unit is natural and reduces main_model.go by ~400-500 lines.
