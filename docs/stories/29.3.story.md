## User Story

As a ThreeDoors user, I want to see which tasks are blocking my work and manage dependencies from the detail view, so that I understand task relationships and can organize my work effectively.

## Context

This story builds on Story 29.1's DependencyResolver to add TUI visualization and management of task dependencies. It covers FR112 (blocked-by indicator) and FR114 (dependency management in detail view) and FR115 (circular dependency rejection with user-visible error).
