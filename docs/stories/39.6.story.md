# Story 39.6: Spacebar as Enter Alias in Doors View

## Status: Done (PR #303)

**Epic:** 39 — Keybinding Display System
**Priority:** P1
**Estimate:** XS (<1 hour)

---

## User Story

As a user, I want to press spacebar to open the selected door, so that the most accessible key on the keyboard performs the most common action without requiring me to move my hand to the Enter key.

## Context

The spacebar is the largest and most accessible key on the keyboard, yet it does nothing in the doors view — the primary interaction screen. An 11-agent party mode panel (PR #298) unanimously recommended making spacebar an Enter alias in the doors view.

Research artifact: [Spacebar Action Debate](_bmad-output/planning-artifacts/spacebar-action-debate.md)

The spacebar already functions as "advance/confirm" in onboarding (`onboarding_view.go:160`) and "page down" in synclog. Making it an Enter alias in the doors view creates consistent semantics across the app: spacebar = advance/confirm.
