## User Story

As a ThreeDoors user, I want tasks with incomplete dependencies automatically hidden from doors and tasks to become available when their dependencies complete, so that every door I see is genuinely actionable.

## Context

This story builds on Story 29.1's DependencyResolver to integrate dependency checking into the door selection pipeline. Two key behaviors: (1) GetAvailableForDoors() filters out dependency-blocked tasks, and (2) when a task completes, any newly-unblocked dependents trigger a doors refresh via DependencyUnblockedMsg.
