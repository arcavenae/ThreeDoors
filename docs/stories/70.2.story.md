## Story

As a ThreeDoors user,
I want to type `:history` and see what I've accomplished,
So that I get positive reinforcement from seeing my completed tasks without leaving the app.

## Background

ThreeDoors helps users DO things — but currently there's no way to reflect on what's been done. The history view fills this gap: a warm, simple list of completed tasks grouped by day. No metrics, no charts, no guilt — just "here's what you did."
